<p><p>The sound-proof cabinets market comprises two main types: closed type and partially closed type. Closed type cabinets are fully enclosed to provide maximum sound insulation, ideal for environments requiring strict noise control, such as recording studios or medical facilities. In contrast, partially closed type cabinets feature openings or mesh to balance sound attenuation and ventilation, making them suitable for situations where some ambient noise is acceptable, such as in offices or light industrial settings. Both types cater to diverse acoustic needs.</p></p>
