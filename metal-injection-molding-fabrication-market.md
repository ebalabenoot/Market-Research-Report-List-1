<p><p>The Metal Injection Molding Fabrication market features two primary types of furnaces: batch and continuous. Batch furnaces process materials in discrete groups, suitable for lower volume production and enabling flexibility in material types and processing conditions. In contrast, continuous furnaces operate in a steady flow, ideal for high-volume production, delivering consistent quality and efficiency. Each furnace type caters to different production needs, emphasizing the balance between versatility and scalability in the metal injection molding process.</p></p>
