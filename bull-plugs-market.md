<p><p>Bull plugs are essential components used in piping systems to seal openings and prevent leakage. The market for bull plugs can be categorized into three main types: threaded, grooved, and butt-weld. Threaded bull plugs feature external threads for easy installation, making them suitable for various applications. Grooved bull plugs use a groove system for quick assembly and disassembly, ideal for flexible piping setups. Butt-weld bull plugs are welded directly to the pipe, providing a robust, permanent seal, suitable for high-pressure environments.</p></p>
