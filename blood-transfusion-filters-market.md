<p><p>The blood transfusion filters market comprises three main types: Whole Blood Transfusion, Platelet Transfusion, and Red Cell Transfusion. Whole blood transfusions involve transferring all components of blood, including red cells, plasma, and platelets. Platelet transfusions specifically target increasing platelet counts in patients, essential for those undergoing chemotherapy or with platelet disorders. Red cell transfusions focus on replenishing red blood cells to treat conditions like anemia or substantial blood loss. Each type utilizes specialized filters to ensure safety and efficacy during transfusions.</p></p>
