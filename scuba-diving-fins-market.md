<p><p>The scuba diving fins market comprises two primary types: open-heeled fins and closed-heeled (full foot) fins. Open-heeled fins feature an adjustable strap that allows for easy donning and doffing, making them ideal for divers who wear dive boots for added insulation. Closed-heeled fins, or full foot fins, provide a snug fit without additional footwear, offering better control and efficiency, particularly in warm water conditions. Each type caters to different diving environments and user preferences, enhancing overall diving experience.</p></p>
