<p><p>The Fluid-Applied House Wrap market consists primarily of two types: Air Barriers and Water Resistive Barriers. Air Barriers are designed to prevent the uncontrolled movement of air into and out of a building, enhancing energy efficiency and indoor comfort. Water Resistive Barriers, on the other hand, protect structures from water infiltration while allowing moisture vapor to escape, thus preventing mold and rot. Together, they provide comprehensive protection against environmental elements, improving overall building performance and durability.</p></p>
