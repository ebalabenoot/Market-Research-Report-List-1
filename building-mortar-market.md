<p><strong>The Building Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Mixed Mortar</li><li>Dry Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building mortar market comprises two primary types: wet mixed mortar and dry mortar. Wet mixed mortar is prepared on-site, requiring water for application, which ensures immediate usability but can be affected by environmental conditions. In contrast, dry mortar is factory-produced and packaged, requiring only the addition of water at the point of use. This type offers consistent quality, ease of storage, and reduced wastage, making it increasingly popular in modern construction applications for its efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
