<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Inductive Type</li><li>Mutual Inductive Type</li><li>Eddy Current Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive displacement sensors are categorized into three main types: Self Inductive Type, Mutual Inductive Type, and Eddy Current Type. Self Inductive sensors measure changes in inductance caused by the presence of nearby conductive materials, making them suitable for close-range applications. Mutual Inductive sensors operate by detecting variations in inductance between two coils, often used in precision applications. Eddy Current sensors utilize induced currents in conductive materials to measure displacement, offering high accuracy without direct contact, making them ideal for high-speed applications.</p></p>
