<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Orthodontic Headgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cervical Pull</li><li>High-Pull Headgear</li><li>Reverse-Pull (Facemask)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthodontic headgear market includes various types tailored for specific dental corrections. Cervical pull headgear exerts force from the back of the head to the teeth, helping to reposition molars. High-pull headgear applies pressure from above, guiding upper teeth into alignment while influencing jaw growth. Reverse-pull headgear, also known as a facemask, directs forward force to the upper jaw, commonly used in cases of underbite. Each type addresses unique orthodontic needs, facilitating effective treatment outcomes.</p></p>
