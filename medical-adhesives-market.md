<p><p>The medical adhesives market comprises various types: </p><p>1. **Water Type**: These are water-based adhesives ideal for skin applications, offering biocompatibility and easy removal.</p><p>2. **Solvent Type**: Utilizing organic solvents, these adhesives provide strong bonding and are often used in surgical procedures, though safety measures are crucial due to toxic fumes.</p><p>3. **Solids**: These adhesives are typically used in devices or applications requiring rigid bonding, offering durability and strength.</p><p>4. **Hot Melt Type**: These are thermoplastic adhesives applied in molten form, providing strong, flexible bonds upon cooling, commonly used for device assembly.</p></p>
