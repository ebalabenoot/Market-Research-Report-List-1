<p><p>The animal parasite prevention and control drugs market includes three primary types: topical, injection, and oral medications. Injection medications offer a systemic approach, providing long-lasting protection against various internal and external parasites through a single dose. Oral medications are administered through ingestible forms, targeting both internal parasites such as worms and external issues, ensuring comprehensive parasite control for pets and livestock.</p></p>
