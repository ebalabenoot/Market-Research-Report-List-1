<p><strong>The Power Management Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Management Unit (PMU) market comprises two main types: centralized and distributed. In a centralized system, power management is coordinated from a single, central unit, enhancing efficiency and simplifying management but potentially creating single points of failure. Conversely, a distributed market involves multiple autonomous units managing their own power needs, promoting resilience and flexibility. This decentralized approach allows for localized decision-making and optimization, catering to specific demands while reducing the risk of widespread outages in the overall system.</p></p>
