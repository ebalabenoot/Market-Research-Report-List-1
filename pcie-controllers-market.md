<p><p>The PCIe controllers market segments into SCSI, SATA, and NVMe, each addressing specific storage needs. SCSI controllers enable traditional enterprise storage solutions, offering high data integrity and reliability. SATA controllers provide cost-effective connections for consumer-grade SSDs and HDDs, focusing on broader compatibility. NVMe controllers leverage PCIe's high-speed lanes, optimizing performance for modern SSDs, ideal for gaming and data-intensive applications. Each type serves unique market demands, balancing speed, capacity, and cost for various computing environments.</p></p>
