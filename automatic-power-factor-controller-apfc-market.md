<p><p>Automatic Power Factor Controllers (APFC) are devices used to optimize power factor in electrical systems, enhancing efficiency and reducing energy costs. The market for APFCs is divided into two main types: Active APFCs and Passive APFCs. Active APFCs employ advanced electronics to dynamically adjust power factor in real-time, providing precise control and compensation for non-linear loads. In contrast, Passive APFCs utilize fixed capacitors and inductors to improve power factor but offer less flexibility, making them suitable for stable loads.</p></p>
