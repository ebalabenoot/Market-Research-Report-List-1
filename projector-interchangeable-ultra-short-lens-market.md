<p><strong>The Projector Interchangeable Ultra Short Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>L(90°)</li><li>Folding(not 90°, not 180°)</li><li>Folding(180°)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Projector Interchangeable Ultra Short Lens market is categorized into four main types based on their design and projection angles. </p><p>1. **Straight Lenses** project images without any angle adjustment, ideal for direct wall or screen placements. </p><p>2. **L(90°) Lenses** allow for projections at a right angle, facilitating flexible installation. </p><p>3. **Folding (not 90°, not 180°) Lenses** enable angled projections that are neither vertical nor horizontal, suitable for unique setups. </p><p>4. **Folding (180°) Lenses** offer the ability to project directly opposite the projector's position, enhancing versatility in space-constrained environments.</p></p>
