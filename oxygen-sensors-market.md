<p><ul><li>Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensor</li><li>Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen sensors market features various types, including Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensors and Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensors. Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensors operate by maintaining a constant voltage to measure oxygen concentration, offering high accuracy and stability in various environments. In contrast, Galvanic Cell Sensors rely on chemical reactions to generate an electrical current proportional to the oxygen level, making them simple and cost-effective. Both types are crucial for applications in automotive, industrial, and environmental monitoring sectors, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
