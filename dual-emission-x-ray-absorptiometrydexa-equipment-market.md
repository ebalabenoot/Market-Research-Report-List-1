<p><p>The Dual Emission X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment market consists of two main types: central and peripheral DEXA bone densitometers. Central DEXA systems are typically used in clinical settings for comprehensive assessments of bone mineral density, particularly in the spine and hips, offering high accuracy and precision. Peripheral DEXA systems, on the other hand, are more portable and are used for quick screenings at various locations, assessing bone density in the forearm, heel, or finger. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing osteoporosis and related conditions.</p></p>
