<p><p>The automotive rubber hose market comprises three primary types: low-pressure hoses, high-pressure hoses, and oil-resistant hoses. Low-pressure hoses are designed for applications involving minimal pressure, typically handling fluids like coolant or air. High-pressure hoses are built to withstand greater pressures, essential for fuel and hydraulic systems. Oil-resistant hoses are specifically formulated to resist degradation from petroleum-based oils, making them suitable for fuel lines and lubrication systems. Together, these hoses cater to diverse automotive needs, ensuring safety and efficiency.</p></p>
