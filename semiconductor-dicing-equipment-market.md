<p><p>The semiconductor dicing equipment market includes various types, primarily grinding wheel dicing machines and laser dicing machines. Grinding wheel dicing machines utilize rotating abrasive wheels to cut silicon wafers into individual chips, suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, laser dicing machines employ focused laser beams to precisely slice through wafers, offering flexibility for complex geometries and thinner materials. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for miniaturization and increased efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
