<p><p>The radiator pipe market comprises three main types: copper radiator tubes, aluminum radiator tubes, and copper-aluminum composite radiator tubes. Copper radiator tubes are valued for excellent thermal conductivity and durability, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Aluminum radiator tubes are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, offering cost and weight advantages. Copper-aluminum composite radiator tubes combine the benefits of both materials, providing efficient heat transfer along with reduced overall weight and manufacturing costs, appealing to a broad range of automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
