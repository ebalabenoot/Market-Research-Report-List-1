<p><p>The commercial food dehydrators market includes two primary types: stackable food dehydrators and shelf dehydrators. Stackable models feature a vertical design where trays can be stacked, allowing for adaptable space and easy expansion of drying capacity. These are ideal for small to medium operations. Shelf dehydrators, on the other hand, have a horizontal layout with fixed shelves, offering consistent airflow and more significant capacity, catering to larger businesses requiring efficient, high-volume drying solutions. Both types enhance food preservation and extend shelf life.</p></p>
