<p><p>The Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) film and sheet market is categorized into two primary types: PMMA film and PMMA sheet. PMMA film is a thin, flexible layer commonly used in applications such as displays, signage, and protective coverings due to its optical clarity and impact resistance. PMMA sheet, on the other hand, refers to thicker, rigid panels utilized in various industries, including construction and automotive, for applications like glazing, lighting, and surface protection, benefiting from its durability and weather resistance.</p></p>
