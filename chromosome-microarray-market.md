<p><p>The chromosome microarray market includes several types: </p><p>cDNA microarrays utilize complementary DNA to analyze gene expression levels, allowing for the comparison of mRNA from different samples. Oligo DNA microarrays use short, synthetic DNA sequences for high specificity in detecting gene variations and mutations. BAC microarrays employ bacterial artificial chromosomes to investigate larger genomic regions, enabling structural variation analysis. SNP microarrays are designed to identify single nucleotide polymorphisms, playing a crucial role in genetic studies and disease association research.</p></p>
