<p><ul><li>Manual Tile Cutter</li><li>Electric Tile Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor tile cutters market consists of two primary types: manual and electric tile cutters. Manual tile cutters are hand-operated tools that utilize a scoring wheel and snapping mechanism for precise cuts, making them ideal for smaller projects or DIY enthusiasts seeking cost-effective solutions. In contrast, electric tile cutters use a diamond blade and water cooling system, offering faster, more efficient cutting for larger jobs and professional use. Both types cater to varying user needs based on project scale and budget.</p></p>
