<p><p>The Aerospace Oxygen System market includes three primary types: Liquid Oxygen Systems, which store oxygen in a cryogenic state for high-capacity use; Gaseous Oxygen Systems, utilizing compressed oxygen cylinders for immediate access during emergencies; and Onboard Oxygen Generation Systems (OBOGS), which produce oxygen from the air onboard, ensuring a continuous supply for flight crew and passengers. Each system serves critical roles in enhancing safety and support during flight operations, catering to various aerospace applications and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
