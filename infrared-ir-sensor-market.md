<p><strong>The Infrared IR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensor</li><li>Quantum Infrared (QIR) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared (IR) sensor market comprises two main types: Thermal Infrared (TIR) sensors and Quantum Infrared (QIR) sensors. TIR sensors detect thermal radiation, enabling them to measure temperature differences and are widely used in applications like surveillance and industrial monitoring. In contrast, QIR sensors utilize quantum effects to detect infrared radiation with higher sensitivity and precision, making them ideal for applications in medical imaging and gas detection. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, enhancing technological capabilities.</p></p>
