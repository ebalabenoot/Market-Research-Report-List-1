<p><p>The sports drinks market is categorized into three types: isotonic, hypertonic, and hypotonic. Isotonic drinks replenish fluids and electrolytes quickly, making them ideal for endurance activities. Hypertonic drinks contain higher sugar concentrations, providing a quick energy boost post-exercise but are less effective for fluid replacement during activities. Hypotonic drinks have lower electrolyte and sugar content, allowing for rapid hydration, making them suitable for light exercise or recovery. Each type serves distinct athlete needs based on activity intensity and duration.</p></p>
