<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) technologies market is primarily categorized into three types: single-sided, double-sided, and multi-layer boards. Single-sided PCBs have components mounted on one side, making them cost-effective and suitable for simple applications. Double-sided PCBs allow for components on both sides, enabling more complex designs and denser layouts. Multi-layer PCBs consist of three or more layers, offering advanced functionality and compactness, catering to high-performance electronics. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries, influencing design choices and production processes.</p></p>
