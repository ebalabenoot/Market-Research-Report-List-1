<p><ul><li>Visibility Tint</li><li>Enhancement Tint</li><li>Opaque Tint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tinted soft contact lenses market features three main types: visibility tint, enhancement tint, and opaque tint. Visibility tint lenses have a light tint for easier handling, maintaining the eye's natural appearance. Enhancement tint lenses, often slightly deeper, enhance the natural color of the eyes without completely altering it. Opaque tint lenses provide a bold change, covering the natural eye color entirely and creating dramatic effects, ideal for color transformation and costume use. Each type serves unique aesthetic preferences and functional needs.</p></p>
