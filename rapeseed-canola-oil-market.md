<p><p>The rapeseed canola oil market primarily consists of two types: cold-pressed canola oil and extracted canola oil. Cold-pressed canola oil is obtained through mechanical pressing without heat, preserving more nutrients and flavor, making it suitable for dressings and gourmet cooking. In contrast, extracted canola oil involves solvent extraction, resulting in a neutral taste and higher yield, commonly used for frying and baking. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, influencing pricing and market demand in the culinary industry.</p></p>
